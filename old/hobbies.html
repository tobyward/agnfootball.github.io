My Other Hobbies

Hockey, Cricket and Geocaching
I know most people reading this will have read 'geocaching' and thought to themselves 'what on earth is geocaching?' So I will explain. Geocaching is a global treaure hunt with millions of people taking part. The idea is that using a GPS receiver (this can now be done using the geocaching app on your mobile phone if it contains GPS functionality) you enter the co-ordinates of the location where the geocache is hidden. Then follow the GPS receiver to the location and then search for the hidden box. Geocaching is a really fun day out with kids or even just to visit new places. For more information visit www.geocaching.com
