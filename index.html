AGN Football started in April 2024 with the idea to create engaging football content for fans around the globe, generating forums for fans to come together to discuss the sport's major incidents and talking points.

Football is a passionate sport laced with excitement, controversy and drama - there is always something to talk about.

As an Arsenal fan, the channel will always include Arsenal related content, however my vision is to unite football fans now matter which team they support.
